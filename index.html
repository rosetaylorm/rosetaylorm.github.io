Welcome to my corner of the web!



I'm a New Yorker with a passion for startups, public policy, and good coffee.

Currently, I am a student at MIT, studying Management Science and Political Science. I direct MIT StartLabs, a student club and non-profit dedicated to promoting collegiate entrepreneurship. I also create content for MIT's Regional Entrepreneurship Acceleration Program, MIT's premier international entrepreneurship program. I have previously interned at Rest Devices, working on everything non-technical from business development to press relations and in the Systems Department of Transatlantic Reinsurance Company. This summer, I will be working with the Department of Education's Office of Education Technology on the ConnectED Initiative.

On the side, I enjoy scuba diving, playing with my golden retriever, and blogging.
